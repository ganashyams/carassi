Exterior

The Toyota Glanza is a hatchback car model that is based on the Suzuki Baleno. It's essentially a rebadged version of the Baleno, produced as a result of the collaboration between Toyota and Suzuki. The Glanza was introduced to the Indian market as part of this collaboration. The Glanza inherits much of the Baleno's design, features, and performance characteristics, with some minor differences in trim levels and equipment. It offers a comfortable and spacious interior, efficient engines, and modern features typically found in hatchbacks of its class.

Interior

Interior Comfort and Features: Inside, the Glanza offers a spacious and comfortable cabin with ample legroom and headroom for passengers. It comes equipped with modern features such as a touchscreen infotainment system, smartphone connectivity, automatic climate control, and steering-mounted controls. Safety: The Glanza is equipped with safety features such as dual front airbags, ABS with EBD, rear parking sensors, and ISOFIX child seat anchors to ensure the safety of occupants. Fuel Efficiency: With its efficient engine options and lightweight construction, the Glanza delivers good fuel economy, making it an economical choice for daily commuting and long drives.

Engine specs

The Toyota Glanza typically offers two engine options: 1. **1.2-liter Petrol Engine**: This engine is a naturally aspirated unit with a displacement of 1.2 liters. It delivers decent power and torque output suitable for city driving and highway cruising. Specific power and torque figures may vary depending on the specific variant and market. 2. **1.2-liter Mild-Hybrid Petrol Engine**: Another engine option for the Glanza is a 1.2-liter mild-hybrid petrol engine. This engine incorporates a mild-hybrid system, which consists of an integrated starter generator (ISG) and a lithium-ion battery. The ISG assists the engine during acceleration and provides energy regeneration during braking, improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. These engine options are designed to offer a balance of performance and fuel efficiency, catering to the needs of different customers. The specific engine specifications, including power output, torque, and fuel efficiency, may vary depending on factors such as the variant, trim level, and market regulations. For precise engine specifications, it's best to refer to the official documentation or website of Toyota or its authorized dealers.
